About Flora

Built for plant lovers, powered by AI

🌱

What is Flora AI?

Flora AI is a real-time plant diagnosis app that uses Google's Gemini AI to identify plant issues from photos. Simply scan a leaf or upload a photo and get an instant diagnosis with actionable solutions.

🧠

How it works

Take or upload a photo of your plant. Our AI analyzes the image, identifies any diseases, pests, or deficiencies, and gives you a clear diagnosis along with simple steps to fix the issue.

📊

Track & Monitor

Save your diagnoses, track plant health over time, log watering, and set reminders — all in one place. Flora helps you stay on top of your plant care routine.

🔒

Your data

All your plant data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or store any personal information. Your photos are only used temporarily to generate a diagnosis and are never saved.
